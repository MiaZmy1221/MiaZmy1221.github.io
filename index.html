About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Ohio State University (OSU). My advisors are Prof. Zhiqiang Lin and Prof. Yinqian Zhang. Before joining OSU, I graduated from Nankai University in China with the Bachelor’s degree of Computer Engineering in 2018.

I am delighted to announce that I will be graduating next summer and seeking academic positions. Look forward to connect with fellow academics, researchers, and potential employers.
